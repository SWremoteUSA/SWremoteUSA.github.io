April 24-26, 2020



Global Online Startup Weekend Unite to Fight COVID-19 USA



Stay Home, Work Together. Change The World.

For the first time ever, Techstars staff and our US-based team of passionate startup community leaders has reimagined every aspect of Startup Weekend and converted everything into a remote, online, high-energy experience that culminates the weekend of April 24-26.

At this online version of Startup Weekend, geography is no longer a limitation. We are taking the best of the in-person experience and amplifying it with the power and reach of the best digital tools available. Just like a normal Startup Weekend, we expect a commitment for the 54 hours of focused work and fun. We’re excited to enjoy this epic weekend with you all! We hope we empower you to make new connections, discover new things, and build something amazing! That’s our mission and we’re here to help.

One Country. Two Sites.

In order to craft a similar experience for everyone, we’ve tried to account for schedules of people in different time zones by creating a site and platform for those in the Eastern and Central time zones (East) and those in the Mountain and Pacific time zones (West). Please sign up accordingly!

Register Here

The action begins when you register! Please visit the following sites for registration and more information:
East: http://communities.techstars.com/usa/usa-east-ny-usa/startup-weekend/16187
West: http://communities.techstars.com/usa/usa-west-ca-usa/startup-weekend/16218

Essential Platforms

We can’t work on distributed teams without a few digital platforms. Essentials for this event include Discord, Zoom and a new platform, What Should We Do. You’ll be given links and more information about each of these after you register.

All in Good Time

Each Startup Weekend has several key elements, but in order to organize this special event, we’ve shifted a few and added some time on the front end to enable the discussions and project exploration that lead to forming the best teams possible. Here’s how our modified schedule runs in 10 easy steps:

1.  Register Now Consider inviting your friends to do the same! (Deadline Wednesday, 4/22 11:59 PM EDT/PDT)
2.  Pitch ideas for problems and solutions
3.  Discover projects and vote on your favorites
4.  Meet attendees and connect with other attendees
5.  Form a team with other people who share your passion
6.  Enroll your team and idea so we can assign a facilitator and help your team (Deadline: Friday, 4/24 1:00 PM EDT/PDT)
7.  Watch the kickoff on Friday night
8.  Work all weekend on your project using our remote tools and techniques
9.  Submit your project for official judging
10. Watch the closing event on Sunday night
